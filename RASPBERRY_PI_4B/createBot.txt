1- Navigate to the https://discord.com/developers/applications.
2- Click on the "New Application" button.
3- Give the application a name and click "Create".
4- Create a Bot User by navigating to the "Bot" tab and clicking "Add Bot".
5- Copy the token.
6- Go to the "OAuth2" tab.
7- tick the "bot" checkbox under "scopes".
8- choose bot permissions.
9- Create the bot, copy and paste the given URL and select the server's you want to add the bot to.
